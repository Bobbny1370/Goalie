About Us

Welcome to Pad Stackers, where passion meets opportunity on the ice.

At Pad Stackers, we believe in:

  • Provide high end coaching for affordable costs.
  • Recognizing potential in every player, beyond predetermined molds.
  • Offering a place for any skill level and age group to improve.
  • Teaching parents and players how to analyze and improve when coaching isn't an option.

Our Services

At Pad Stackers, we offer personalized goalie coaching to meet every player's unique needs. Look at our services and reach out to sign up.

1. Private Lessons - $35

Elevate your goaltending skills with one-on-one private lessons. Our experienced coaches work closely with you, focusing on your individual strengths and areas for improvement to bring out the best in your game.

2. Semi-Private Sessions - $40(2)/$45(3)

Experience personalized attention in smaller groups with our semi-private sessions. Work closely with a coach alongside 2 or 3 goalies, allowing for tailored guidance and collaboration in a focused setting.

3. Video Analysis - $20

Gain valuable insights into your performance with our video analysis service. Break down your moves, techniques, and strategies with expert coaches, enabling you to refine your skills and make informed improvements.

4. Season Plans - $100

Prepare for success with our season planning service. Our coaches will help you strategize and set goals for the season, ensuring you have a structured plan to maximize your development and performance.

5. Remote Coaching - $20

No matter where you are, our remote coaching service brings expert guidance to your fingertips. Stay connected with your coach, receive personalized training plans, and access valuable insights to enhance your goaltending skills.
