About Us

Welcome to Pad Stackers, where passion meets opportunity on the ice.

At Pad Stackers, we believe in breaking down barriers. Hockey shouldn't be exclusive; it should be a chance for every aspiring player to thrive. My coaching philosophy is rooted in the belief that financial constraints should never hinder someone's pursuit of excellence on the ice. I've witnessed firsthand the impact of missed opportunities due to financial constraints, and I'm committed to ensuring that every dedicated player gets the chance they deserve.

In addition to affordability, I value the potential in every player. I've seen instances where coaches overlooked talented individuals, simply because they didn't fit a predetermined mold for higher-level play. At Pad Stackers, we believe that everyone deserves a chance to develop and improve, regardless of initial assessments. Every player, no matter their skill level, deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Join Pad Stackers, where we're not just coaching goalies; we're fostering a community where passion, dedication, and opportunity converge to shape the future of hockey.

Our Services

At Pad Stackers, we offer personalized goalie coaching services to meet the unique needs and aspirations of every player. Our commitment is to provide quality coaching, foster a love for the game, and make hockey accessible to all. Explore our services below:

1. Private Lessons - $35

Elevate your goaltending skills with one-on-one private lessons. Our experienced coaches work closely with you, focusing on your individual strengths and areas for improvement to bring out the best in your game.

2. Semi-Private Sessions - $40(2)/$45(3)

Experience personalized attention in smaller groups with our semi-private sessions. Work closely with a coach alongside 2 or 3 goalies, allowing for tailored guidance and collaboration in a focused setting.

3. Video Analysis - $20

Gain valuable insights into your performance with our video analysis service. Break down your moves, techniques, and strategies with expert coaches, enabling you to refine your skills and make informed improvements.

4. Season Plans - $100

Prepare for success with our season planning service. Our coaches will help you strategize and set goals for the season, ensuring you have a structured plan to maximize your development and performance.

5. Remote Coaching - $20

No matter where you are, our remote coaching service brings expert guidance to your fingertips. Stay connected with your coach, receive personalized training plans, and access valuable insights to enhance your goaltending skills.
